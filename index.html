ClairMaunt Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

At ClairMaunt, a brand of ClairMaunt Solutions, tagline "Your Brand In Every Hand", we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of the personal and business data you share with us. We handle your brand identity and data with the highest standards of integrity, discretion, and care.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you request quotations, upload design files, contact us, or interact with our communication channels.

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, mobile number, job title, and shipping/billing address.
  • Business Information: Company name, industry type, GSTIN or local tax identifiers, event details, delivery locations, marketing objectives, target audience, budget, quantity, and order frequency.
  • Uploaded Files: Brand logos, design files, custom label graphics, and QR code marketing materials.
  • Automatically Collected Data: IP address, browser type, referral URL, page views, and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate custom design mockups, calculate pricing quotes, manage production timelines, and arrange shipping logistics for customized bottle orders.
  • To generate and print functional QR codes for menus, social media, catalogs, and event landing pages.
  • To administer our "Advertise On Bottles" program and coordinate sponsorships.
  • To process quotation requests, contact form submissions, and communicate via email, phone, or WhatsApp.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data. We share information only with trusted service providers who perform operations on our behalf (cloud database hosting, payment processing, shipping/logistics companies). Finalized layouts are shared with our BIS-certified bottling partners to print labels and package your customized bottles, subject to strict confidentiality controls.

4. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security practices, including SSL/TLS encryption for all site forms and uploads, to protect your personal and design files. However, no electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your logo assets, mockups, and business information for as long as necessary to fulfill orders, facilitate easy customer re-orders, or comply with legal requirements. You may request data deletion at any time.

6. User Rights & Grievance Compliance

In compliance with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, the SPDI Rules 2011, and GDPR guidelines, you have the right to access, rectify, restrict, or request the deletion of your personal data. You may withdraw your consent at any time.
